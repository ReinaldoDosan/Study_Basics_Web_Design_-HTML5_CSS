Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners an anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses in asana (proper alignment and posture) pranayma (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate ro advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement - you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically througth yoga poses.
Restoratice Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experienvce. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
